Dear Riti,

I'm deeply sorry for all the pain,
For every tear and every strain.
I know your parents are on your mind,
We'll take it slow, love unconfined.

We'll meet but rarely, not each day,
No daily calls to pull away.
I regret the steps that hurt us both,
With you, I find my happy growth.

These two days, I've lost my way,
No sleep, no food, in disarray.
I want the best, your joy, your light,
To spend the present, hold you tight.

I love you so much.

Do you forgive me, Riti? My baccha please?

Thank you, Baby!

I love you and I promise to never fail you.
